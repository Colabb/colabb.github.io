About me

I am A Graphic Designer who was born in Serang, 3 September 2000. Born in a humble family who grew up in an environment that did not allow him to become a Graphic Designer. Until sitting in high school can get knowledge about the basics of design, video editing, audio, and animation. And I became interested in sharpening my views on Graphic Design. At the time of vocational school, I learned more self-taught through the internet, and I began to get a path that was in line with my fashion and style in designing a visual. In improving my knowledge and ability in designing a visual, I am supported by many online mentors and friends of the design community on Facebook who have more knowledge about Design. So that I can learn more apart from lessons from school.

I am a person who really enjoys my work in designing a visual in communicating information in it. Of course, with the support of the work area that allows me to get more inspiration in conceptualizing a design.
